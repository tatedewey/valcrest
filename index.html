About Valcrest Capital

Valcrest is a boutique investment bank that combines institutional-grade rigor with boutique agility to empower lower middle-market companies through strategic capital solutions that create lasting value.

Our advisory board brings over 50 years of combined experience and has executed $97B+ in capital markets transactions, including IPOs, follow-ons, and private placements. Entrepreneurs first, bankers second - we offer the sophistication of a large bank with the agility of a founder-led shop.

By integrating AI and data intelligence, we accelerate outreach, improve matchmaking, and deliver superior execution. We partner with visionary entrepreneurs and growth companies determined to reach their peak potential.

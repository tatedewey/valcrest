Capabilities

Debt products
across the capital stack

We work across the full range of multifamily debt structures — from stabilized agency executions to ground-up construction and structured preferred equity.

01

Bridge & Transitional Financing

Short-term debt solutions for acquisitions, lease-up, and value-add repositioning. We source bridge capital from debt funds, banks, and credit facilities aligned to your business plan timeline.

02

Construction & Development Loans

Ground-up and substantial rehabilitation financing for multifamily and build-to-rent projects. We access construction lenders — including family offices and debt funds — for complex development capital.

03

Permanent & Agency Financing

Long-term fixed-rate executions through agency programs and balance sheet lenders for stabilized multifamily assets. We optimize proceeds, rate, and structure for your hold strategy.

04

Mezzanine & Preferred Equity

Structured junior capital to bridge the gap between senior debt and equity. We place mezzanine and preferred equity with institutional capital partners for sponsors seeking higher leverage.

About Valcrest

Built for multifamily.
Focused on execution.

Valcrest Capital is a commercial real estate debt brokerage exclusively focused on multifamily transactions. We work with real estate sponsors, developers, and investment companies navigating acquisitions, ground-up development, and value-add repositioning.

Our approach is direct and relationship-driven. We go to market with precision — targeting the right lenders for each transaction rather than broadcasting to the broadest possible audience. The result is faster response, tighter pricing, and better execution for our clients.

We are deliberate about the engagements we take on. Every client relationship receives senior-level attention throughout the debt placement process. Our network extends beyond domestic lenders to include international private capital sources, giving us additional reach for transactions where conventional lenders fall short.

Lender Relationships

Direct access to debt funds, banks, insurance companies, and family offices actively deploying into multifamily.

Execution Speed

We move with urgency. Our lender network allows us to generate meaningful market response within days, not weeks.

Multifamily Specialist

Our exclusive focus on multifamily means deeper lender relationships, sharper market knowledge, and better outcomes for sponsors.
